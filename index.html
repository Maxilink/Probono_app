Frequently Asked Questions

Is Probono Desk really free for users seeking legal aid?

Yes, absolutely! Probono Desk is completely free for individuals seeking legal aid. Law firms on our platform provide pro bono (free) legal services as part of their commitment to social responsibility.

How do you verify law firms on the platform?

We require all law firms to submit their CAC registration documents, practicing certificates, and professional credentials. Our verification team conducts thorough background checks before approving any law firm to join the platform.

What types of legal issues can I get help with?

Our platform covers a wide range of legal practice areas including family law, employment law, landlord-tenant disputes, small claims, immigration, criminal defense, and more. You can select your specific issue when submitting a case.

How long does it take to get a response from a law firm?

Most cases receive responses from law firms within 24-48 hours. However, response times may vary depending on the complexity of your case and the availability of lawyers in your area.

Can I communicate directly with the lawyer handling my case?

Yes! Once a law firm accepts your case, you can communicate directly with the assigned lawyer through our secure messaging system. All communications are encrypted and confidential.

What if I'm not satisfied with the legal help I receive?

We have a quality assurance process in place. If you're not satisfied with the service, you can report the issue to our support team. We also maintain ratings and reviews to ensure accountability among law firms.
